Elevate Your Arcade Experience

Revamp your arcade machine effortlessly with our services:

1) Modernize Your Cabinet

With the plug-and-play control panel set that we sell, you can upgrade your old machine! This includes a new board, power supply, and wired Jamma harness. After installation, you'll have access to 60 or more game titles for your enjoyment! and you'll have the choice to play with either Joystick or Trackball.

2) EPROM Excellence

If you have a machine in need of a new eprom, give us a call! We can program a new eprom specific for your machine. due to old manufacturing practices of certain companies, if you would like to order a replacement EPROM, we request that you send a photo of your old EPROM so we can make sure that you get the right one.

Note that the installation of the wrong EPROM can lead to your motherboard damaging.

3) Expert Board Repairs

We specialize in expert board repairs! Our shop has all the tools to get your board running correctly. Just send the board and our experts will take care of the rest!

some of the boards we repair include:

  • Jamma boards
  • Non-Jamma boards
  • Chassis boards
  • Power Supply boards
  • MPU boards
  • Driver boards

4) CRT Monitor Rejuvenation

We specialize in monitor rejuvination! With the advancement of technology, some have resorted to using LCD or LED Monitors. But We find that the original CRT Monitors show the best quality, and were made to fit in your arcade cabinet. Before you decide to upgrade that monitor, Consider Rejuivnating it to restore the color, and brightness which can save you time and money!

Rediscover the thrill of arcade gaming with our cutting-edge solutions. Contact us today!
